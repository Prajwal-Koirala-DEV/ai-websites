# Free AI Tools

This document provides a list and comparison of 25 free AI tools. These tools range from conversational agents to creative generators, offering diverse functionalities for various needs.

---

# Tools Review

1. **[ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/)**

   - **Overview**: OpenAI's conversational model with broad capabilities. It generates human-like text responses, assists in code generation, and provides text completion services.

2. **[Google Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/)**

   - **Overview**: Google's conversational AI designed to provide information and assistance. It integrates with Google services and helps with tasks such as finding information and setting reminders.

3. **[Perplexity AI](https://perplexity.ai/)**

   - **Overview**: A creative AI tool that generates text, poetry, and stories. It is used for creative writing, storytelling, and content generation.

4. **[Claude AI](https://claude.ai/)**

   - **Overview**: An AI artist that creates unique art pieces based on user input. It generates art, images, and designs using AI algorithms.

5. **[Pi AI](https://pi.ai/)**

   - **Overview**: An AI-powered personal assistant that helps with daily tasks and information retrieval. It manages tasks, provides information, and assists users with various queries.

6. **[Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/)**

   - **Overview**: A platform for AI models and natural language processing tools. It offers a wide range of AI models, NLP tools, and resources for developers and researchers.

7. **[Meta AI](https://meta.ai/)**

   - **Overview**: An AI platform offering various AI tools and services, including creative AI tools, conversational agents, and other AI services.

8. **[Jasper](https://www.jasper.ai/)**

   - **Overview**: Jasper is an AI writing assistant designed to help with content creation, copywriting, and marketing materials. It offers text generation and completion services.

9. **[Copy.ai](https://www.copy.ai/)**

   - **Overview**: Copy.ai provides AI-driven content generation for marketing copy, social media posts, and ad copy. It is geared towards creative and marketing professionals.

10. **[Writesonic](https://writesonic.com/)**

    - **Overview**: An AI-powered writing tool that helps generate marketing content, blog posts, and product descriptions. It is useful for content creators and marketers.

11. **[Runway ML](https://runwayml.com/)**

    - **Overview**: Runway ML offers AI tools for creative professionals, including tools for image and video editing, as well as machine learning model deployment.

12. **[DeepL Translator](https://www.deepl.com/translator)**

    - **Overview**: DeepL provides advanced AI translation services with high-quality translations in multiple languages. It is useful for translation and language learning.

13. **[ChatSonic](https://chatsonic.com/)**

    - **Overview**: ChatSonic offers conversational AI and chatbot services for customer support, lead generation, and user engagement. It provides text-based interactions and integrations.

14. **[Scribe AI](https://scribe.ai/)**

    - **Overview**: Scribe AI assists with note-taking and summarization. It helps users generate concise summaries and detailed notes from various types of content.

15. **[Kuki](https://www.kuki.ai/)**

    - **Overview**: Kuki is a conversational AI chatbot designed for customer support and engagement. It provides interactive and personalized responses.

16. **[Replika](https://replika.ai/)**

    - **Overview**: Replika is an AI companion that engages in conversations to provide emotional support and companionship. It offers personalized interactions and chat experiences.

17. **[DALL·E](https://www.openai.com/dall-e/)**

    - **Overview**: DALL·E, developed by OpenAI, generates images from textual descriptions. It is useful for creative professionals and artists.

18. **[Runway](https://runwayml.com/)**

    - **Overview**: Runway offers a suite of AI tools for creative projects, including video editing, image generation, and machine learning model deployment.

19. **[Lumen5](https://www.lumen5.com/)**

    - **Overview**: Lumen5 is an AI-driven video creation tool that converts text content into engaging videos. It is useful for marketing and content creators.

20. **[Artbreeder](https://www.artbreeder.com/)**

    - **Overview**: Artbreeder allows users to create and modify images using AI. It is used for generating unique art, portraits, and other visual content.

21. **[Fotor](https://www.fotor.com/)**

    - **Overview**: Fotor provides AI-powered photo editing and design tools. It helps with image enhancement, collage creation, and graphic design.

22. **[Zoho Writer](https://www.zoho.com/writer/)**

    - **Overview**: Zoho Writer is an AI-enhanced word processing tool that offers text generation, grammar checking, and collaborative writing features.

23. **[Grammarly](https://www.grammarly.com/)**

    - **Overview**: Grammarly provides AI-powered grammar and writing assistance. It offers spelling, grammar, and style suggestions for improving written content.

24. **[Tome](https://tome.app/)**

    - **Overview**: Tome is an AI-powered writing assistant that helps with creating and formatting documents, presentations, and other written content.

25. **[Genius AI](https://www.genius.ai/)**

    - **Overview**: Genius AI offers a range of AI tools for data analysis, natural language processing, and machine learning applications. It is used for various AI-driven insights and tasks.
